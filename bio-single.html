Anusha is a dynamic force in human-focused design who effortlessly bridges the disciplines of development and design. Her in-depth knowledge of front-end web development is paired with an instinctive ability to balance user psychology, resulting in digital journeys that are both functional and captivating.

With roots embedded in the diverse culture of Karnataka, India, Anusha now calls Calgary home, and she integrates her diverse cultural experiences into her design philosophy.

Beyond professional work, Anusha is a community advocate, serving as a Usability Testing Team member for the City of Calgary and IncluCity Calgary. As the Team Lead for Creative Media at IncluCity Calgary, she led to a 30% increase in IncluCity's user engagement.

Versatility is her forte, seamlessly transitioning between disciplines to adapt to changing project requirements. Anusha is passionate about design research, human-centered design, and storytelling, with the goal of making the digital world more user-friendly and pleasant.

Away from the screen, she explores hiking trails in the Rockies with her husband, crafts healthy meals, and enjoys simple pleasures like walks for her favorite mango bubble tea.

“Creativity, empathy, and a dedication to enhancing the digital world for all are the driving forces behind my design and development mission.”
