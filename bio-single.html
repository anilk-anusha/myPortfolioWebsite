About me

Hello👋 I'm Anusha. Welcome to my website! I am a Multifaceted Full Stack Developer based in Calgary, Alberta with a unique background in UX design, web development and technical writing.

I excel in creating high-quality software that aligns with industry best practices. As a proven collaborator with exceptional communication and conflict-resolution skills, I thrive in team environments and foster strong dynamics with stakeholders. My professional skills encompass a wide range of programming languages, UX design guidelines, libraries, frameworks, databases, and version control systems, allowing me to tackle complex projects with ease.

Drawing from my UX knowledge, I understand the psychology of user behavior and are adept at crafting intuitive, user-friendly applications. I also have the ability to merge the technical intricacies of Full Stack Development with the art of creating captivating interactions. This results in applications that not only meet functional requirements but also enchant users through seamless and delightful digital journeys.

Beyond my professional endeavors, I'm an active volunteer in my community. I've served as a Usability Testing Team Member for the City of Calgary and IncluCity Calgary, where I facilitated test sessions and optimized testing scripts to enhance efficiency. As a Team Lead for Creative Media at IncluCity Calgary, I contributed to a 30% increase in user engagement through engaging social media content.

One of the key advantages I bring to the table is versatility. My ability to seamlessly move between these disciplines means I can adapt to changing project requirements and contribute effectively at various stages of product development. This versatility results in efficient workflows and a holistic approach to projects, saving time and resources while ensuring the highest quality in documentation and user experience.

Thank you for visiting my website, I hope you enjoy exploring my portfolio just as much I enjoyed crafting these experiences. Let's collaborate and make the digital world even better together!😄
